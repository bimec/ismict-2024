Refund/Cancellation Policy

All refund requests must be made in writing to Professor Sandra Dudley dudleyms@lsbu.ac.uk must be received no later than May 1st, 2024. For those who pay by credit card, refunds can only be processed to the credit card to which the original charge was made. Processing of the refunds may take over 30 days. No refunds will be processed for cancellations after May 10, 2024.
