Mission

Internet of Medical Things (IoMT), healthcare cybersecurity, biomedical informatics, and molecular communication and computing for medicine are the focus of the 16th International Symposium on Medical Information and Communication Technology (ISMICT) to be held in Lincoln, Nebraska, USA, May 2-4, 2022. The theme is New Dimensions into Healthcare and Medicine.
