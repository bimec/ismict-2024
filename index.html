Scope

Internet of Medical Things (IoMT), healthcare cybersecurity, biomedical informatics, and molecular communication and computing for medicine are the focus of the 18th International Symposium on Medical Information and Communication Technology (ISMICT) to be held in London, UK, on May XX-XX, 2024. The theme is ICT in Precision Medicine: Paving the Cyberspace in Diagnostics, Therapeutics, and Drug Development. Papers are invited on topics including, but not limited to, the following:

  • AI/Data Biomedical Analytics
  • Bioinformatics and Systems Biology for Medicine, Healthcare and Welfare
  • Bio/Molecular Communication and Computing for Medicine
  • Body Area Network (BAN) Technologies
  • Communications Systems for Medical Applications
  • Cybersecurity in Healthcare
  • Information Theory and Processing for Biomedicine
  • Internet of Medical Things and Bio-Nano Things
  • Medical Device Regulatory Science
  • Nanocommunications and Nanonetworking for Nanomedicine
  • Privacy and Security Issues
  • Wearable and Implantable Devices
  • Wellness and Sports Training
  • Pervasive Health Care and Patient Monitoring
  • Medical/molecular imaging, PET, CT, MRI
  • Brain machine/computer interface
  • Robotics for surgery operation and rehabilitation
  • Sleep analysis
  • Data science for medical big data
