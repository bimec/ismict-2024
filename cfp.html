Scope of the Papers

Internet of Medical Things (IoMT), healthcare cybersecurity, biomedical informatics, and molecular communication and computing for medicine are the focus of the 18th International Symposium on Medical Information and Communication Technology (ISMICT) to be held in London, UK, on May 15-17, 2024. The theme is Remote and On Person Intelligent Monitoring: Real Care For Me Systems. Papers are invited on topics including, but not limited to, the following:

  • Pervasive Health Care and Patient Monitoring
  • Microwave Sensing Techniques for Medical Applications
  • Bio/Molecular Communication and Computing for Medicine
  • Body Area Network (BAN) Technologies
  • Cybersecurity in Healthcare
  • Internet of Medical Things
  • Privacy and Security Issues
  • Wearable and Implantable Devices
  • Wellness and Sports Training
