The Courtyard Lincoln Downtown/Haymarket encompasses the Historic Haymarket charm in the heart of downtown Lincoln, Nebraska. Located within walking distance of the University of Nebraska campus, Pinnacle Bank Arena, Memorial Stadium, Haymarket Park, Canopy Street & The Railyard, The Courtyard Lincoln Downtown/Haymarket is centrally located and allows you to experience many of the highlights Lincoln, Nebraska has to offer.

Set in the Historic Haymarket, this modern hotel is a 7-minute walk from the University of Nebraska and a 4.8-mile drive from Lincoln Airport.


Lincoln Airport (LNK)

Lincoln Airport is a public/military airport five miles northwest of downtown Lincoln, the state capital, in Lancaster County, Nebraska, United States. The airport lands you in 10 major international airports and keeps air travel as friendly as the skies it flies. Fly nonstop to Atlanta, Chicago, Denver or Minneapolis!

Directions to hotel from Lincoln Airport

By Car: You can rent a car at any of the car rental services a the airport and drive to the hotel which is just 4.8 miles/10 minute drive away.

By Taxi: You can book for an Uber or Lyft.

By Bus: Alternatively, if you want to use public transport, you can take the bus on route 52 towards West Fletcher and NW 12, which is from the airport to Gold's - 11th Street and then walk for 5 minutes to the hotel.

Omaha Eppley Airfield (OMA)

Eppley Airfield, also known as Omaha Airport, is an airport in the midwestern United States, located three miles northeast of downtown Omaha, Nebraska. The airport lands you in 10 major international airports and keeps air travel as friendly as the skies it flies. Fly nonstop to Austin, Atlanta, Chicago, Dallas, Denver, Houston, Las Vegas, Minneapolis, Nashville, Orlando or more!Omaha Eppley Airfield (OMA) is just 65 miles or around an hour drive far from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln.
